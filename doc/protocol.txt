Reset
=====
Cube pulses DIRSTB, drive asserts DIDSTRB and holds it till ready.

Command
=======
Rise DIDSTRB at 9th byte, wait for DIDIR & !DIHSTRB, clock data out on falling edge of DIDSTRB, GC acks by asserting
DIHSTRB before the last 3 bytes.
Drop DIDSTRB when ready for next command.

Cover
=====
DICOVER low on bootup = GC will try to read before starting the boot animation.
